# 2nd Act Capital — Brand

**What it is:** A private membership platform for post-liquidity, high-character founders and operators deploying capital thoughtfully. Register: *premium private club, not fintech startup* — Soho House meets a boutique family office. Earned wealth, operator sensibility, understated luxury, discretion, community.

## Voice
Sophisticated but approachable. Confident, quiet, precise. No hype, no exclamation marks, no growth-hacky copy. Short, considered sentences. Words like *members, allocation, discretion, register, co-invest* — never *users, crush it, unlock, supercharge*.

## Color (use exactly these)
- Navy `#1B2B4B` — primary: structure, headings, nav, dark panels
- Gold `#C5A880` — accent: marks, emphasis, hairline rules
- Gold Light `#E8D5A3` — highlight: on-navy accent, active/hover
- Background `#FAF9F6` — warm cream canvas, used throughout
- Text `#0F172A` — body ink on cream
- (Nav icons on navy: resting `#9AA6BF` → active `#E8D5A3`)

## Type
- **Display / headings:** Spectral (Google Fonts) — weights 300/400/500/600 + italic. Editorial serif with warmth.
- **Body / UI:** Hanken Grotesk (Google Fonts) — 400/500/600/700. Base size **17px**.
```
https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Spectral:ital,wght@0,300;0,400;0,500;0,600;1,400&family=Hanken+Grotesk:wght@400;500;600;700&display=swap
```

## Logo
Wordmark: **"2ⁿᵈ Act"** in Spectral (navy) above **"C A P I T A L"** in Hanken Grotesk, uppercase, ~0.5em letter-spacing, gold. The serif/sans + navy/gold contrast distinguishes the two halves. On navy: name in cream, descriptor in gold-light.

## Icon mark — "The Ascent"
Three rounded squares rising on a diagonal; the highest is gold-light, the lower two gold — capital built block by block, the second act ascendant. Primary = gold on a navy rounded-square seal. Reversed = navy on cream. Circular crop for member avatars.

## Iconography
Bespoke line set, 24px grid, 1.6px strokes, rounded joins, generous negative space. SPV Manager = a sealed legal document (contract/entity), not a chart. Keep the whole set in this one stroke family.

## Avoid
Fintech tropes (bright greens, Robinhood/Stripe gradients), heavy law-firm serifs, generic financial clip-art (dollar signs, bar charts as primary marks), dark mode (the app is light/cream throughout), emoji, rounded-corner-with-left-accent-border cards.
